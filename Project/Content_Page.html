Social Science

Social science is the study of human society and social relationships. It covers a wide range of topics that seek to understand various aspects of human behavior and culture. Empirical methods are used in social science disciplines to collect data, analyze patterns, and develop theories to explain and predict social phenomena. We will learn about the history of places, objects, and events all over the world in this subject.
